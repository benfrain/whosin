About In/Out

Use In/Out to organise who’s “In” and who’s “Out” of your regular team-based sports or activities.

Edit, add or choose event names from the header.

Add, edit and move people ‘In’ and ‘Out’ using the name slats.

All data is stored in your browser. To back-up or restore your data, click the Load/Save icon in the footer.
